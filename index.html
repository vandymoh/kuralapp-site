About Kural

Kural means voice in Tamil — a reference to the Thirukkural, a classical Tamil text on ethics and wisdom. The name reflects the belief that every person has something to say.

Kural is built by a parent, for families navigating nonspeaking communication. It is currently in early access via TestFlight.
